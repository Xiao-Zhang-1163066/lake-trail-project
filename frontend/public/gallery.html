Submission tips

Best experience for all

  • Use landscape photos where possible (at least 1600px wide).
  • Include a caption — e.g. location, time of day, or the story behind the shot.
  • By sharing you confirm you captured the image and have consent from any people featured.

Questions?

Email manager@wet.org.nz if you’d like help resizing photos or removing an image.
