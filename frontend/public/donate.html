Help Te Waihora thrive

Every contribution sustains restoration work, trail stewardship, and community programmes that keep Te Waihora healthy for future generations.

Give via Givealittle Donations are processed by Waihora Ellesmere Trust. You’ll receive a tax receipt and project updates.

Where your gift goes

  • Planting natives and maintaining predator control networks.
  • Building safe boardwalks, signage, and trail infrastructure.
  • Supporting mana whenua-led education and volunteer programmes.

Restore wetlands

Your donation funds planting days, weed control, and monitoring that improve habitat for birds and tuna.

Equip volunteers

Provide tools, kai, and safety gear so the Te Waihora whānau can continue hands-on mahi throughout the year.

Share the story

Help deliver cultural interpretation, guided experiences, and learning resources for schools and visitors.

Why your support matters

Te Waihora Trail reconnects people with wetlands, wildlife, and the stories of kaitiaki who care for this landscape. Donations ensure the trail remains safe, accessible, and rich with biodiversity.

Your support keeps volunteers supplied, enables hands-on education for rangatahi, and protects habitats for species such as kārearea and the Australasian bittern. Together we’re building a legacy of kaitiakitanga around the lake.
